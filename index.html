CodeWars!

CodeWars is the first competition the CompSciClub here at PHS usually attends first, and is one we typically go to for fun and not to win as it is very challenging and competitive.

Competion break down

Each team is given a packet of questions in which they read the problem, test sample input, and submit what they think the solution is to the judges

Time limit: 3 Hours Number of questions: 20
